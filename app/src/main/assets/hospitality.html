Bangladesh is known abroad as a country of floods, cyclones and other natural calamities. But if you meet any foreign tourist, who has been in Bangladesh for some time, you will certainly be amazed to hear about the positive sides of the country. All the people of this country are very helpful to the tourists. Their friendly behaviour towards the people of the outside of the country will be surely surprised the people of the whole world. Even today Bangladeshi people cry at the departure of their guests. They are connected to the tourist emotionally. The people of Bangladesh are very simple and down to earth. People will surely enjoy their visit to Bangladesh.
